Explanatory Note

 

This Amendment No. 1 to Form 10-Q (this “Amendment Filing”) amends the Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q of Hospira, Inc. (the “Company”) for the quarter ended September 30, 2012, which was originally filed on November 7, 2012 (the “Original Filing”). The Company is filing this Amendment Filing solely for the purpose of amending Item 6 of Part II of the Original Filing and filing the Business Transfer Agreement, dated August 29th, 2012, by and among Orchid Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Ltd., Mr. K. Raghavendra Rao, and Hospira Healthcare India Private Limited, including the exhibits (the “Agreement”), as Exhibit 2.1, and not as Exhibit 10.2 as originally filed.

 

This Amendment Filing does not modify or update any other part of the information set forth in the Original Filing, other than filing the Agreement as Exhibit 2.1, and not as Exhibit 10.2 as originally filed.
