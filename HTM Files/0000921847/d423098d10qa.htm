EXPLANATORY NOTE

Hudson City Bancorp, Inc. (the “Company”) is filing this amendment (“Form 10-Q/A”) to its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended June 30, 2012, filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on August 8, 2012 (“Form 10-Q”), solely for the purpose of amending Item 6 of Part II of the Company’s Form 10-Q to add the Company’s Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation as an exhibit and to update the exhibit list.

This Form 10-Q/A should be read in conjunction with the original Form 10-Q, which continues to speak as of the date of the Form 10-Q. Except as specifically noted above, this Form 10-Q/A does not modify or update disclosures in the original Form 10-Q. Accordingly, this Form 10-Q/A does not reflect events occurring after the filing of the Form 10-Q or modify or update any related or other disclosures.
