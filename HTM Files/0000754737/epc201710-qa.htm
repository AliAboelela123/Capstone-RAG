Explanatory Note
 
On July 2, 2009, Amendment No. 1 to the Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q of SCANA Corporation ("SCANA") and South Carolina Electric & Gas Company ("SCE&G") for the quarter ended June 30, 2008 (the "Form 10-Q") was filed to amend Exhibit 10.01 thereto (the “Exhibit”) in connection with SCANA’s and SCE&G’s confidential treatment request for certain portions of the Exhibit. On May 24, 2017, the Public Service Commission of South Carolina ("SCPSC") revoked the confidential treatment that had been afforded the Exhibit by the SCPSC. As a result, this amendment No. 2 is being filed to provide the Exhibit in its entirety and to revise the related description in the Exhibit Index to remove references for confidential treatment previously sought pursuant to Rule 24b-2. No changes are being made to any other portions of the Form 10-Q originally filed.
